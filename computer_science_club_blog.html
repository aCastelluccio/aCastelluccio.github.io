Club Update for the Month of April

A Look at the Current Situation May 1, 2018

Hey everyone, if you’re reading this I appreciate it! We have big things planned for the Computer Science Club next semester. As you can see from the picture, we finally became a real club! Our official advisor is Emily Hill, and with all of the annoying administrative work finished we can move on to actually trying to be an awesome club. We have some really cool events we’d like to try out next semester, and we’re looking forward to getting to meet all of you and have you participate.

ANINIVERSARY OF DREW'S FIRST HACKATHON

A look back, April 23, 2018

It feels like forever ago that we were able to host our first hackathon. I remember being worried that no one would show up, and even worse that no one would stay. But fortunately we ended up getting a nice turnout, and actually had an awesome day! We had a lot of cool submissions and ideas, and it was interesting to see what people came up with. Shotout to our winner, Drew and his cool mathmatical drawing using python, Bobby our second place winner with an awesome take on battleship, and Manny the third place finalist with his (insert later)!
